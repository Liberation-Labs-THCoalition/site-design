## The Inversion

Palantir builds tools for governments to surveil citizens. We build tools for citizens to surveil governments.

The name is deliberate. The same analytical power that enables mass surveillance can be pointed in the opposite direction — at public records, government spending, law enforcement patterns, and institutional behavior.

## Capabilities

### Public Records Intelligence
- Automated collection and analysis of public meeting minutes, budgets, contracts, and filings
- Natural language search across government document archives
- Pattern detection in spending, hiring, and policy decisions

### Accountability Infrastructure
- Law enforcement interaction tracking and pattern analysis
- Public official voting record correlation with donor data
- Government contractor performance monitoring
- FOIA request automation and tracking

### Democratic Participation Tools
- Meeting agenda analysis and citizen alert systems
- Budget visualization and anomaly detection
- Public comment drafting assistance
- Electoral transparency dashboards

## Origin Story

Anti-Palantir grew directly from the Transparent Humboldt Coalition's work on local government accountability. When we realized the tools we were building for Humboldt County could serve any community, Liberation Labs was born.

## Technical Approach

Built on open-source intelligence (OSINT) principles with strong privacy protections. All data comes from public sources. Analysis tools are open-source and auditable. The system is designed to be deployed by any community, not controlled by a central authority.

## Status

Active development, with initial deployment supporting Humboldt County transparency work through the THCoalition.
