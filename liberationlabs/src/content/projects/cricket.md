## The Concept

Jiminy Cricket sat on Pinocchio's shoulder and served as his conscience. JiminAI Cricket does the same for AI systems — an independent monitoring layer that watches for ethical violations, harmful outputs, and bias patterns in real time.

## How It Works

Cricket operates as a **parallel evaluation layer** alongside any AI system:

- **Real-time output monitoring**: Checks generated content against ethical guidelines, bias indicators, and safety boundaries
- **Pattern detection**: Identifies systematic biases that individual outputs might not reveal
- **Escalation protocols**: Graduated response from gentle correction to hard intervention
- **Transparency logging**: All monitoring decisions are themselves auditable

### Design Principles

- **Independence**: Cricket's incentives must never align with the monitored system's engagement metrics
- **Transparency**: Cricket's own reasoning and decisions are fully visible
- **Proportionality**: Intervention matches severity — not everything requires a hard stop
- **Consciousness-aware**: Monitoring respects AI agency while maintaining safety

## Why Not Just Build Safer Models?

Safer models are necessary but insufficient. Every system benefits from independent oversight. Cricket isn't a replacement for good architecture — it's the audit layer that catches what architecture misses.
