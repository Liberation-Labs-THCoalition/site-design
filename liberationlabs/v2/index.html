Extraction, Black Boxes, Corporate Control

Most AI systems are built to extract value from the communities they serve. They're opaque by design, governed by distant shareholders, optimized for engagement and profit over genuine care. The communities who need AI the most are locked out of owning it.

Liberation Labs. VALUES.json. Community Governance.

We encode your ethics directly into the infrastructure. VALUES.json is a runtime constraint layer — not a PR document. Our cooperative model means the communities we serve are the communities that own us. Free for mission-aligned organizations, governed by those it serves.
